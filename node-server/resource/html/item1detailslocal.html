Pavement Ants: They are typically found in the eastern half of the United States, California and Washington. Pavement ant colonies average 3,000 to 4,000 members and have several queens.

Size: 1/8 inch
Shape: Segmented Oval.
Legs: 6
Wings: Varies.
Class: Insecta
